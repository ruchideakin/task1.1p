Join Us for Garba Night!

Event Details

Date: October 10th

Time: 7:00 PM - Midnight

Location: Community Hall

Come dressed in your best traditional attire and get ready to dance the night away!

How to Participate

  1. Get your tickets in advance or at the door.
  2. Bring your friends and family for a night of joyous garba dancing.
  3. Enjoy traditional Gujarati music and live performances.
  4. Don't forget to bring your dancing shoes!

Event Highlights

  • Delicious Snacks and Drinks
  • Prizes for Best Traditional Outfits
  • Lively Dance Performances
